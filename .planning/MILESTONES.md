# Milestones

## v1.0 MVP (Shipped: 2026-02-16)

**Phases completed:** 5 phases, 10 plans, ~20 tasks
**Lines of code:** 2,850 Swift
**Timeline:** 2 days (2026-02-15 to 2026-02-16)

**Delivered:** A fully functional local-only iOS birthday reminder app with contact import, configurable notifications, group management with two-way Contacts sync, and home/lock screen widgets.

**Key accomplishments:**
- Contact import with SwiftData persistence and one-tap sync from iOS Contacts
- Notification engine with 64-notification ceiling management and configurable delivery time
- Group management with bidirectional Contacts sync and per-group notification preferences
- Home screen and lock screen widgets via WidgetKit with shared app group data
- Widget polish: uniform alignment, concise inline text, 4-entry medium widget, and app icon
